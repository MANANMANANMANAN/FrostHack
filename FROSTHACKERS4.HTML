Methods To Overcome Tiredness
  • Dehydration can contribute to feelings of fatigue, so drink plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Physical activity can boost your energy levels and improve your overall mood. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days of the week.
  • The blue light emitted by screens can interfere with your sleep quality. Try to avoid electronic devices like smartphones, tablets, and computers at least an hour before bedtime..
  • If you're feeling tired during the day, take short breaks to rest and recharge. Even just a few minutes of relaxation can make a difference.
