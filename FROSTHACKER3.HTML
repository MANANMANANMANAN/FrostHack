Methods To Overcome Sleeplessness
  • Take some slow, deep breaths and focus on the sensation of the air moving in and out of my body.

  • Listen to calming music or nature sounds to create a soothing atmosphere.

  • Write down any thoughts or worries that come up.

  • Try getting out of bed and doing a quiet activity in another room, like reading a book or doing a puzzle, until you feel tired again.
