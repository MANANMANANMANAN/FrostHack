Methods To Overcome Pyrexia(Illness)
  • Over-the-counter fever reducers such as acetaminophen (Tylenol) or ibuprofen (Advil, Motrin) can help lower fever and relieve discomfort. Follow the recommended dosage instructions and consult with a healthcare provider if you have any concerns or underlying medical conditions.

  • Soaking in a lukewarm bath can help lower body temperature and provide comfort. Avoid cold baths, as they may cause shivering and raise body temperature.

  • Wash your hands frequently with soap and water, cover your mouth and nose when coughing or sneezing, and avoid close contact with sick individuals to prevent the spread of illness and reduce your risk of developing a fever.
